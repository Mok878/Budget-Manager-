                About Budget Manager

                Budget Manager is a simple, intuitive tool designed to help you take control of your personal finances. Whether you're trying to save for a big purchase, pay off debt, or just understand where your money goes each month, we're here to help.

                Why Budget Manager?

                • Easy to Use: No complicated setup or learning curve
                • Organized Categories: Track expenses by housing, food, transportation, and more
                • Privacy First: All your data stays in your browser - we don't store anything on servers
                • Free Forever: No subscriptions, no hidden fees

                How It Works

                1. Add your income sources (salary, freelance work, etc.)
                2. Enter your bills and expenses by category
                3. Set your savings goals
                4. Watch your budget balance automatically
